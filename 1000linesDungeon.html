Polished Dungeon Crawler

Controls

Desktop:

Movement: Arrow Keys or WASD

Inventory: I

Magic: M

Help: ?

Restart: R

Cast Spell: 1-4 (after selecting spell)

Close Menus: ESC

Mobile:

Movement: Tap arrows or tap grid

Actions: Tap buttons (🎒, ✨, ?, 🔄)

Use Item: Tap item in inventory

Drop Item: Long-press item in inventory

Game Mechanics

Combat: Bump into enemies to attack.

Hunger: Decreases every 10 turns. Eat food (🍄) to restore.

Magic: Press ✨ to select a spell, then 1-4 to cast.

Stairs (🪜): Descend to the next level.

Traps (⚠️): Triggered when stepped on.

Items: 💊 = Potion, 💰 = Gold, ⚔️ = Weapon, 🛡️ = Armor, 🍄 = Food

Theme: Tap 🌓 to toggle dark/light mode.
